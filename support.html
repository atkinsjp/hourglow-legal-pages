Common questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open Settings inside HourGlow and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Where is my data stored?
All data stays on your device and syncs privately through your iCloud account. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open the Settings app on your device → tap your name → Subscriptions → HourGlow.
