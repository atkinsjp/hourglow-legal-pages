Privacy Policy

HourGlow · Last updated April 26, 2026

We believe your family's data belongs to you. HourGlow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data, including child profiles and timer history, is stored locally on your device and synced via your private Apple iCloud account. We have no access to your data.

What we store

  • Child profiles you create (names, ages, avatars, optional photos)
  • Timer history and session statistics
  • App preferences and settings

All of this data is kept on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced privately through your own Apple iCloud account using Apple's end-to-end infrastructure.

What we don't do

  • We do not run analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to advertisers or third parties.
  • We do not have servers that hold your family's information.
  • We do not collect personal identifiers, contacts, or location data.

Photos

If you choose a photo for a child profile, that image is stored locally on your device (and in your iCloud, if enabled). HourGlow only accesses the photos you explicitly select via the system photo picker.

Subscriptions

HourGlow Plus subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to verify the status of your subscription. Apple and RevenueCat may receive an anonymous purchase identifier as part of this process. We never receive your payment information.

Children's privacy

HourGlow is designed for use by parents and guardians. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Profile data created for children stays on the parent's device.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the app.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out at support@atkins-media.com.
